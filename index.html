SHEET 002 / PROFILE

Engineering meets Software.

I started out in electrical power engineering — load calculations, distribution design, the fundamentals of how a building gets its power. Somewhere along the way I got more interested in writing the software that does that work than doing it by hand, and moved into BIM automation and .NET development. Now I build Revit add-ins, lighting-calculation engines, and shop-drawing automation — the kind of tools that sit between an engineer's design intent and a set of construction documents. I keep one foot in computer graphics and 3D visualization too, since a surprising amount of good BIM tooling is really a geometry problem in disguise.

Based in — Cairo, Egypt
Currently — ITI, AEC Informatics Track
Background — B.Sc. Electrical Power & Machines Eng.
